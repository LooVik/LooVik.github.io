CityGo

CityGo.

CityGo is a game you play on your phone that uses real locations and maps. You can play mini-games like Snake and collect points. It's designed to help you relax and feel better by walking around. The main idea is to show that games can make you feel less stressed and help you stay healthy. Right now, we're testing the game on different phones. If you want to try it out, you'll need to download it on either an iPhone or an Android phone using Unity.
