NaturalExplorer.

This is a OpenGame World Level Design using Unity and C#. Player have to explore the natural and will be able to look over kinds of nature such as flowers, trees, grass and etc. Player must move around and wonder around to observe what is surround by them. This is now still yet a Level Design and on-going to make it as a game to collect n woder around.
