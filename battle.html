Burglar Battle

A splitscreen heist battle with two teams of two skilled robbers compete against each other to be the first to loot the vault. players must solve puzzles, complete challenges, and evade monsters as they race to pull off the ultimate heist. This is a project for Playstation 5 game made with Unity

Being developed by up to 50 students which divided into few small groups.
